# MVP Rules Locked (Checkpoint 3)

## Hard Constraints

-   Only PDF upload (max 10MB)
-   No JPG/PNG
-   Login required (Supabase)
-   No email verification
-   No password change
-   No account deletion
-   No account settings page

## UX Rules

-   RU / DE switch at login
-   Default language: RU
-   Auto analysis after upload
-   No preview of document
-   No progress bar
-   Auto-save always enabled

## Flow Rules

-   Only 1 active case at a time
-   Case can be cancelled with confirmation
-   Case resumes after re-login
-   Questions are dynamic AI-driven
-   One question at a time
-   Back button allowed

## Output Rules

-   Final answer shown on screen
-   Download PDF + DOCX
-   No editing allowed

## Pricing Rules

-   Free: 1 document
-   Paid: 3,99€ per document OR 9,99€/month
-   Payment is simulated

## Data Rules

-   History always stored
-   No deletion of completed cases
